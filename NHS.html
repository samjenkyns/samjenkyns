S.T.O.P. is the acronym you need to remember to help reduce infections and to protect your friends and family.

If you follow the steps in the S.T.O.P. acronym, you can be sure that you can help protect others and stop the spread of COVID-19.

The latest COVID-19 varient (named‘VUI – 202012/01’) is around 71% more transmissable than the previous strain of the virus.

It is vitally important that you stay at home and help protect the NHS and save lives.


Download the NHS COVID-19 app here. Find out what tier your area is in.

S - Symptoms.

Check your symptoms and see if you need to self-isolate.

T - Test.

If you have symptoms, book a free test via the NHS app or the Government website.

O - Outbreak.

Stop the outbreak of Coronavirus by self-isolating at home and only leaving to get tested.

P - Protect.

Protect others by maintaining social distancing, wear a mask or face covering, washing or sanitizing your hands reguarly and downloading the official NHS COVID-19 App.
